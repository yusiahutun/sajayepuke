The 12 Best Eyebrow Hold Gels of 2024, Tested and Reviewed

If you’ve been on BeautyTok lately, then you’re well aware that one of the biggest TikTok trends of the year is #fluffybrows — which, by the way, has over 168.5 million mentions on the app. For those that don’t know, fluffy brows are eyebrows that have been brushed up and set in place to look fuller. Where some people require extra strokes from an eyebrow pen or pencil, others can achieve the look with nothing more than an eyebrow hold gel. No matter which camp you fall in, having a trusty clear brow gel in your kit is the trick to nailing this face-framing makeup trend. 

Like most makeup products, though, many eyebrow hold gels exist. Because of this, it can be difficult to know which actually performs well. That’s where we come in. A group of 20 InStyle reviewers tested 23 of the most popular clear brow gels and ranked them on a scale of 1 to 5 in four key categories, including application, feel, performance, and overall look. 

Some of their less stellar notes detailed sticky textures and dry-feeling brows, to seemingly cemented brow hairs and off-putting scents. Naturally, those clear brow gels didn’t make the cut. Twelve did, though, including Anastasia Beverly Hills Strong Hold Clear Brow Gel, which came out on top as the best overall.

Our Testing Process

To start, we spent about a week researching the top-rated clear eyebrow hold gels on the market. After honing in on online ratings, we spent 24 hours putting the 22 most popular gels to the test. We had each of our testers try the eyebrow hold gels so that they could analyze the performance of each one and write down their insights during the process. In doing so, our testers rated each gel on a scale of 1 to 5 for four key categories: Feel, application, performance, and overall look. Once the data was collected, we were able to narrow down the original pool of products to the 12 best eyebrow hold gels.

What to Keep in Mind

Ingredients

Not all brow gels are made with the same ingredients. As such, it’s helpful to know a few standout ingredients that can work a world of good for the appearance of your arches. Generally speaking, if you want healthy-looking definition, you’ll want to ensure that the formula of your choice is infused with some type of conditioner (like aloe vera, glycerin, and/or castor oil). Kimiko The Brow Sensei and W3ll People Expressionist Clear Brow Gel are both great conditioning options if you want to keep your brow hairs looking soft yet defined. 

Wear Time

If you’re someone who likes to apply their makeup and not think about it until you get back home after a long day, it’s essential that you choose a long-wearing brow gel formula. Otherwise, you’ll have to keep your tube on hand for midday touch-ups. Most brow gels will hold for six to eight hours, but some (including Makeup By Mario Master Hold Brow Gel and Anastasia Beverly Hills Strong Hold Clear Brow Gel) can last for up to 12 hours.

Finish

If you want natural-looking arches, opt for formulas that tout a natural finish (Hourglass Arch Brow Shaping Gel and Joey Healy Brow Structure Clear Set are great options). If you prefer a more intentionally made-up look, choose formulas that are described as silky or polished. One of our favorites is MAC Brow Set.

Your Questions, Answered

How do you apply clear brow gel?

In short: You swipe it on. “A little goes a long way,” Blair says. “I always lightly remove any excess gel from the brush before applying.” 

As far as applying it goes, Holmes says you can wear it on its own or over your favorite brow pencil, pen, or powder. “Brush the product up and out, creating a fan effect,” she says. “Taper the tails according to preference with the tip of the brush or smooth down with your finger.”

Celebrity brow expert Joey Healy tacks onto this, noting that there are three main concepts to keep in mind when applying clear brow gel. “You want to apply the gel vertically in the front of the brow, up and out on a 45-degree angle for the middle, denser part of the brow, and then kind of pinch the tail together at the end to avoid leaving any gaps at the end of the brow,” he explains.

What does clear brow gel do?

The beauty of clear brow gel is that it can be used to achieve the exact arch shape you want, even if your brows don’t naturally lay that way. “You can set the brows in place in your existing shape, or you can create a feathery full look by setting the brows upward, giving them the laminated look that's so popular,” Blair says. 

In short, it’s a must-have, Healy says. “Clear brow gel allows you to style the brows and organize the hairs,” he explains. “A lot of times, people think they need to trim the hair a lot in order to tame the brow, but in reality, they just need a bit of brow gel to keep everything in place.”

Is clear or tinted brow gel better?

That’s a personal preference. If your primary goal is to fluff and set your brows, go with clear; if you’re looking to create a denser look, a tinted option might better suit you. “Clear brow gel is like applying a chapstick, while tinted brow gel is like applying red lipstick — they both cover the lips and have similar benefits, except one is much quicker and requires less attention to detail,” Healy says. “I will say, sometimes it’s better to just use a pencil or powder to fill in the sparse areas of the brow and create your shape, and then go in with the clear brow gel to lock everything in place."
