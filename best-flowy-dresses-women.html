Best Tiered Dress

The Somerset Collection by Anthropologie The Somerset Maxi Dress

Best Tiered Dress

The Somerset Collection by Anthropologie The Somerset Maxi Dress

Credit: Anthropologie

Anthropologie calls this flowing dress its best-selling and best-reviewed dress of all time. Believe the hype: The cascading tiers and relaxed sleeves have an undone effortlessness that naturally attract compliments. "I get stopped in the street by people who want to know where I bought it," one shopper raves.

Try the original version in cotton or, for summer, consider this linen update in nine shades and standard, plus, and petite sizes. Reviewers say the relaxed fit can run large, so consider going down a size.
